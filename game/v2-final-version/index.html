How To Play

Two players compete to fill their cup and hydrate their fish by rolling die to reach 100 points.

  • Each turn, roll the dice to earn points.
  • Roll as many times as you like, but rolling a 1 loses your turn, and rolling two 1's loses your entire score
    (SNAKE EYES)
  • Choose to save your points and pass the turn
  • First player to reach 100 points saves their fish, click start to begin the race!
